ABOUT ZION NATIONAL PARK

Zion National Park is an national park located in southwestern Utah near the town of Springdale. It is the first National park founded in Utah. Zion is known for it’s beautiful views and allotment of fun and exciting activities offered around the park. Come and enjoy a unique experience you will be sure to never forget! Follow the paths where ancient native people and pioneers walked. Gaze up at massive sandstone cliffs of cream, pink, and red that soar into a brilliant blue sky. Experience wilderness in a narrow slot canyon. Zion’s unique array of plants and animals will enchant you as you absorb the rich history of the past and enjoy the excitement of present day adventures.
